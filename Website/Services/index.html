Personalized Programmes

PERSONALISED PROGRAMMES, EXPERT ADVICE


As well as gaining a healthy, strong and toned physique, you will find you have increased energy levels, and that you feel more motivated and enthusiastic in everyday life.


Our personalised transformation programmes combine one-on-one expert personal training with detailed nutritional guidance, mentorship and accountability coaching, 24/7 contact and advice, and everything you need to smash your goals.

The Undergroundfitness body transformation process can be defined in five important steps...


1. Thorough body assessment

Our friendly personal trainers kick-start your body transformation journey with a thorough physical assessment. They measure your body composition and fat percentage, and run a full movement analysis and screening. This data is then used to set your targets and create your own personalised, step-by-step, life-changing programme.



2. Nutrition and supplementation

Whether you are looking to lose weight, build muscle or tone up, proper nutrition is essential. Your dedicated qualified nutritionist will provide you with an eating plan that has been specifically tailored to your body and goals. Plus, if you don’t fancy cooking for yourself, we can set you up with one of our partners, to have healthy and delicious meals delivered right to your door.



3. One-on-one coaching

Embody’s state-of-the-art Olympic-standard gyms come complete with cutting-edge equipment and world-renowned British coaches. Private and exclusive, there’s never more than 10 clients on the floor at once, and you’ll always have plenty of room and constant attention from your personal trainer.



4. Rehab, sports therapy and massage

Injuries can stop you achieving sustainable results and creating the body you desire. Our highly skilled sports and massage therapists will keep your physical health in optimum condition throughout your body transformation programme, and ensure that nothing stands in your way.



5. Constant monitoring

Every few weeks, your trainer and nutritionist will assess your results and adjust your programme to make sure you stay on track. By constantly focusing on your body transformation results, we ensure that you keep making progress. It can also be hugely encouraging to see how far you’ve come in measurable facts and figures.
